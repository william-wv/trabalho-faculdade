Hey there. I'm Robert and I'm a creative developer. I've been in the industry since 2018 and I work in the intersection between generative art and creative coding with some sparkles of web3 on top.

I've been working with top notch clients in the likes of Gucci, Google, Adidas, Longines, Valentino, LG, Sap, MaxMara, Metamask, Blitz and creative agencies as Immersive Garden, OddCommon, Demodern, Outpost, Wildlife, DotDotDash, Antinomy, Dentsu and more.

In 2023 I hosted an IRL workshop at Harbour university in Barcelona pretending I know WebGL.

I'm currently working as a freelancer and open for collaborations long and short term.
